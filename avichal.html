About

Where Law
Meets Technology

I'm an Advocate enrolled with the Bar Council of Delhi and a member of the Delhi High Court Bar Association, currently pursuing my PGIP-LLM in Insolvency Laws at National Law University, Delhi.

With a Minor in Artificial Intelligence from IIT Ropar, I bring a rare blend of legal acumen and technical fluency. I build AI-assisted tools for legal analysis, automate repetitive legal workflows, and translate complex legal-technical questions into structured, actionable outputs.

My work sits at the intersection of governance design, risk mitigation, institutional capacity, and evidence-driven AI policy — contributing to both national and global policy discourse through published research and practical tool-building.
